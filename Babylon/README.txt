This utility supports 3D models with extension (.babylon), so to convert 3D models of type .obj, .fbx etc,
1.	You need to create a free account on clara.io
2.	Then, upload your model there, press continue, then press create.
3.	Then, click download located on the right side and select Babylon.js (.babylon)
4.	Then, the 3D model will get downloaded.

Steps to include the model in the utility:
1.	In line no. 203, replace the mesh name “sofa2.babylon” with your mesh name(.babylon file).
2.	For texture, proceed to line 211 and replace Aqua.jpg with your texture.
